## 5. Understanding probabilistic patterns

### What this means in your framework:
Not prediction.
Not forecasting.
It means:
- understanding the distribution of behaviors across states and regimes

### Function in your system:
It answers:
- “What behaviors are likely under specific structural conditions?”
Not:
- “what will happen next”
But:
- “what tends to happen when the system is in this configuration?”

### Why this matters:
Because stochastic systems do NOT have:
- fixed outcomes
- deterministic paths

They have:
- conditional likelihoods of behavior

So you are shifting from:
- prediction thinking
to:
- conditional structure thinking
